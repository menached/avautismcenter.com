Becoming a Certified Autism Center

Verbal Pathways is dedicated to supporting minimally verbal adults. Our strategy combines three foundational principles:

  • Autism Certification - Ensuring our program meets the highest standards for autism care.
  • Contextual Learning - Fostering real-world social interactions and shared experiences.
  • Specialized Support - Providing tailored assistance for those with specific needs.

Addressing Unique Needs

In a world where the additional needs of profoundly autistic individuals often go unrecognized, many community based programs lack the appropriate and targeted specialized services. needed. Verbal Pathways fills this gap, focusing on the unique support that profoundly autistic individuals require.

Specialized Care

Our team provides dedicated care for non-verbal and minimally-verbal young adults, ensuring a supportive and understanding environment.

Commitment to Autism Certification

As a Certified Autism Center (CAC), our program is rooted in best practices. We offer an inviting safe and inclusive space where all participants feel valued and supported.

Contextual Learning for Social Growth

We embrace contextual learning, where shared experiences become a foundation for social interaction and communication, helping participants build lasting connections and confidence.

Autism-Trained Facilitators

With our trained facilitators, participants are guided in social interactions to develop familiarity, comfort, and confidence in various situations.

Life Skills Development

By combining the principles of contextual learning and specialized support in a CAC environment, Verbal Pathways helps profoundly autistic individuals develop essential life skills and achieve personal goals.
